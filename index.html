About, sources and privacy

Purpose and limits

CoolSheet is a UNSW academic research prototype for education, research and early feasibility comparisons.

Results use typical weather, model assumptions and user inputs. They are not site measurements, guarantees, certified designs, financial quotes or professional advice.

Responsibility and privacy

Verify all inputs, equipment data, costs, schedules, site constraints and conclusions before relying on a result.

Calculator inputs are stored in this browser so they remain after a page refresh.

Data sources

Weather
PVGIS typical meteorological year data from the European Union Joint Research Centre.
Location
OpenStreetMap geocoding and map data.
Water temperature
BC-Aus model fitted from Burch & Christensen (NREL) and legacy CER/SRES references. It is not AS/NZS 4234:2021 engineering data.
Emissions
DCCEEW National Greenhouse Accounts Factors 2025.
