One site and 19 days cannot validate annual output, other industries, PV cooling gain or long-term performance.

Recommended next model changes

Preserve the scientific models; add an explicit delivery layer.

  • Add an optional, clearly labelled thermal field-performance factor, default 1.00. Offer 0.60–0.75 only as a SOAC-like aquatic sensitivity until more sites and seasons are available.
  • Add aquatic-centre plant availability or pump operating hours. The campaign generated for only 110.8 of 456 hours.
  • Allow the supply model to use pool-return inlet temperature rather than mains water when evaluating a pool-heating array.
  • Keep Model A and Model B coefficients unchanged and separately report gross collector potential, available plant heat and heat actually matched to demand.
  • Collect at least a full year and another installation before changing national defaults. Add PV metering before claiming electrical validation.
