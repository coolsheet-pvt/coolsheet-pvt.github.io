    PVT electrical cooling gain Needs direct field testing against a comparable uncooled PV system. Cooling gain = cooled PVT electricity − uncooled PV-only electricity
    Cooling gain (%) = cooling gain ÷ PV-only electricity × 100
    How the model calculates it
    1. NOCT estimates the uncooled PV panel temperature.TPV = Tair + (G ÷ 800)(NOCT − 20)
    2. The water temperature rise gives the heat removed.Q = ṁcp(Tout − Tin)
    3. Removed heat reduces the estimated PVT panel temperature.TPVT = max(Tin, TPV − Q ÷ (ULA))
    4. The panel temperature coefficient converts the temperature difference into an electricity difference.fT = 1 + γ(Tpanel − 25)

    Both cases use the same sunlight, panel area, system losses and inverter efficiency. The hourly differences are added across the year.
