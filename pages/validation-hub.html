Important limits

These results support comparison and early feasibility work, but they are not certified forecasts.

  • Not yet field validated: PVT cooling gain and full-year PVT performance.
  • Industry demand and operating schedules remain scenario based.
  • Costs, savings and payback depend on site-specific assumptions.
  • Site shading, faults, downtime and equipment ageing are not modelled directly.
