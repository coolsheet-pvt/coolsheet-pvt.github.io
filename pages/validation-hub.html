CoolSheet evidence guide

What has been validated—and what has not?

Start here to understand which CoolSheet results have strong independent support, which have early field evidence, and which should be treated as scenarios.

Overall: suitable for early feasibility work.

The solar and PV supply calculations compare well with independent tools. Thermal field evidence is promising but limited to one monitored site and period. Industry demand, economics and PVT electrical cooling remain assumption-sensitive.

Important limits

Not yet field validated

  • Full-year PVT performance and degradation

Scenario results—not certified forecasts

  • Industry demand and operating schedules
  • Costs, tariffs, savings, payback and NPV
  • Site shading, outages, controls and plant availability
